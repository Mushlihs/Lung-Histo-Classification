Lung Tissue Image Classification

Lung Cancer

Advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques are commonly used for lung tissue image classification. These methods enable automated analysis of medical images, helping medical professionals make informed decisions quickly and accurately.

Lung tissue image classification is a crucial task in medical imaging and cancer diagnosis. It involves analyzing images of lung tissues to determine whether they contain cancerous cells or not. This process plays a vital role in early detection, accurate diagnosis, and effective treatment planning for lung cancer.
